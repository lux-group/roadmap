key_initiatives:
  - heading: A Truly Global Luxury Experience
    description: Expand localisation across the app so international customers see a
      more relevant, trustworthy and seamless experience. This includes
      foundations like language, currency, date formats and region-specific
      content, helping more customers discover and book with confidence.
  - heading: Effortless Booking, End-to-End
    description: Make the journey from discovery to purchase smoother by reducing
      friction across key booking moments. This includes improving high-drop-off
      steps, creating better handover points between app and website, and making
      webview-based flows feel more connected and native.
  - heading: Smarter Discovery & Search
    description: Refresh the way customers search and discover travel so finding the
      right escape feels faster, easier and more intuitive. This includes
      improving the core search experience, leaning further into Natural
      Language Search, and bringing more discovery journeys natively into the
      app.
  - heading: Personalised Inspiration & Content
    description: Make the app a stronger destination for inspiration by bringing
      together more personalised offers, editorial content and curated
      recommendations. The goal is to give customers more relevant reasons to
      return, even when they are not yet ready to book.
  - heading: The App as a Travel Companion
    description: Grow the app beyond booking into a more useful companion throughout
      the travel journey. This includes surfacing clearer trip details, timely
      travel updates and more accessible support, so customers rely on the app
      before and during their trip.
