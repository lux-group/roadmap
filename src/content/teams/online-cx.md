key_initiatives:
  - heading: "AI-Powered Search Relevance"
    description: "Our on-site search is the primary discovery mechanism for millions of travellers. We are deploying a transformer-based semantic search model that understands natural language queries — think 'romantic overwater bungalow for two' rather than keyword matching. Combined with personalised ranking signals, early A/B tests show a 28% improvement in search-to-booking rate and a significant reduction in zero-result searches."
  - heading: "Mobile App Checkout Redesign"
    description: "Mobile accounts for 61% of our traffic but only 43% of our bookings — a gap that represents significant revenue opportunity. We are redesigning the end-to-end mobile checkout flow to reduce steps, surface saved payment methods earlier, and introduce a streamlined guest checkout path. The redesign is informed by 1,200 hours of usability testing and is projected to lift mobile conversion by 26%."
  - heading: "Buy Now Pay Later Integration"
    description: "Affordability is a key purchase barrier for higher-value travel packages. We are integrating BNPL as a first-class payment option, surfaced at the point of intent as well as at checkout. Our commercial analysis shows BNPL-eligible packages have a 34% higher average order value and that offering instalment options converts 22% of otherwise abandoned carts. Full rollout is planned for Q3 alongside a dedicated marketing push."
