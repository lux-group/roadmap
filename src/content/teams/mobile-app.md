  - heading: "Native Style Webviews"
    description: "Make webview flows feel native by improving navigation, showing cart visibility, and using native UI elements so customers don't feel they have left the app. This reduces friction and drop-off while allowing the team to scale new verticals faster without requiring fully native builds. High impact on both friction reduction and conversion across multiple product verticals."
  - heading: "Seamless Website Handover"
    description: "Many customers begin their journey on the app and complete their purchase on the website. Making that handover as easy as possible is critical for conversion. Use tools like Airdrop and pre-auth to allow customers to continue on the website exactly where they left off on the app. This reduces cognitive friction and improves overall conversion and retention across the unified customer journey."
  - heading: "Persistent Customer Support Chat"
    description: "Make active support chats persist and be accessible from any screen so customers can get help without losing their place in the app. This reduces effort and support friction, allowing customers to ask questions mid-journey and continue browsing or booking without abandoning the chat. Improves both retention and reduces support contact spikes during peak booking periods."
