key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Payment Schedule Optimisation"
    description: "Develop a comprehensive payment schedule suite to optimize the offer for customers, focusing on checkout conversion rate and cash flow optimization. This includes lead-time-based experimentation, deposit holds offered to the full customer set, and data-driven tuning of service fees and payment timing. By providing flexible payment options that match customer preference, we expect to drive a 10% improvement in checkout to purchase conversion rates while improving overall cash flow."
  - heading: "Wex VCC and Commercial Optimization"
    description: "Migrate from Citi VCCs to Wex VCCs for Custom Offers, Cruises, and other high-value transactions, capturing better rebates (0.1% uplift for AU offers, 1.7% uplift for non-AU offers) and enabling faster tier-up for higher rebate brackets. This shift, combined with optimized CITI flow and exclusivity deals, targets $500k - $800k per annum in additional rebate revenue. Wex VCC deployment will be complemented by optimizing existing commercial agreements."
  - heading: "Stripe Invoice Elimination via Charge Engine"
    description: "Eliminate Stripe manual invoicing across multiple use cases including split payments, group payments, custom offer deposits, and purchase credit. By moving these transactions to our Charge Engine system which directly charges customer payment methods, we eliminate $200k+ in annual Stripe fees while improving the customer experience through faster payment processing. This addresses a critical blocker for the new Finance ERP system integration."
