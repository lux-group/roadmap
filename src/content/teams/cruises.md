initiatives_section_title: "Key Initiatives"
key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Expand Promo and Inclusion Supply"
    description: "Approximately 70% of cruise total transaction value comes from promo or inclusion-led deals, yet we currently surface only about 50% of available promos versus competitors like Cruise Guru. We are revamping the Admin Portal UI and automating daily competitor deal scraping and supplier promo email ingestion to help the commercial team scale deal creation. This includes improving inclusions error detection and reporting to strengthen operational processes. We are also building an AI free-text internal search tool that surfaces real-time metrics, historic performance, and margin data by cruise line to accelerate negotiations and unlock stronger inclusions and pricing."

  - heading: "Bundling for Flash Supplies Growth"
    description: "Bundling cruises with other supply verticals enables dry cruise rates with no markup, unlocking stronger customer value and differentiated packages. We are starting with hotel bundling, which currently represents about 6% of cruise orders, and building internal tooling to generate bundled Flash deals faster and at scale. Flash deals deliver approximately 13-16% margin versus 8-10% for standard Cruisebank supply. By accelerating Flash deal creation and reducing onboarding time by more than 50%, we can increase the share of higher-margin offers significantly."

  - heading: "Optimize Discovery Journey and Conversion"
    description: "We are improving the discovery journey on site and through communications to drive material conversion uplift. This includes search ranking optimization where cruise landing pages are ranked by conversion and margin logic rather than manual curation, improving image quality to showcase destinations and cruise ships, and gathering customer testimonials to increase email engagement. We are also improving cabin selection flow to reduce the 72% drop-off at this critical funnel stage by simplifying comparison and reducing choice overload."
