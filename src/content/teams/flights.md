initiatives_section_title: "Key Initiatives"
key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Flight + Land Deal Bundled Flow"
    description: "A significant portion of our customers need to book flights along with accommodation, but we do not currently offer bundled flight and land pricing. We are building a bundled booking flow where flights are recommended based on the customer's destination, followed by the ability to add accommodation in a single order. This allows us to leverage bundled flight pricing which are generally priced more competitively compared to standalone flights. Other OTAs like Flight Centre and Booking.com in the AU market already offer this. Force Bundle is mature in our UK market. This feature will allow flights to use wholesome fares at scale and reduce pricing perception issues."

  - heading: "Price Alerts and Search Intelligence"
    description: "Customers tend to search for flights roughly 3-6 months before their next holiday, but we do not currently support customers well during the research phase. We are building price alert capabilities so customers can receive email notifications when fares drop for their searched routes. Additionally, we are introducing flight price trends that help travelers understand whether the current airfare is cheap, typical, or expensive relative to historical prices for the same route and dates. These features reduce booking uncertainty and improve customer confidence."

  - heading: "Ancillary Monetization in Checkout"
    description: "We currently display a limited range of fare classes and do not offer ancillary products like seat selection, baggage, lounge access, or meals at the point of flight booking. We are building ancillary upsell interfaces in both the checkout flow and MyEscapes post-booking management that present relevant add-ons contextually. This includes extending capabilities across ATPCO, NDC, and LCC content types. Based on industry data, customers who engage with seat selection convert at significantly higher rates, and ancillary revenue represents a significant incremental opportunity on flight bookings."
