initiatives_section_title: "Key Initiatives"
key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Dynamic Bundles with Full Vertical Integration"
    description: "We are building dynamic bundling capabilities with full functionality to enable bundling accommodations with Cruises, Flights, and Experiences. This includes bedbank support and market-aware discounting using Price Parrot to determine external prices relative to LE costs and dynamically adjust property-level discount configurations. We are also supporting post-purchase upgrades for refundable bookings approaching check-in, offering customers superior rooms or packages with higher margin value. These features capture incremental margin from customers who are psychologically more willing to upgrade after booking."

  - heading: "Expand Bedbank and Direct Supply"
    description: "We are executing a multi-pronged supply expansion strategy combining direct outreach to property partners, integration with new supply channels, and identifying supply gaps across key destinations. This includes expanding direct supply through chains like Accor and Hyatt via Derbysoft, new villa supply through platforms like Guesty and Lazudi, and niche bedbank connections via TGX with partners like Jalan and Zinantis. We are also developing a fully-featured vacation rental vertical with both direct and bedbank supply, complete with price parity and strong merchandising capabilities."

  - heading: "Accommodation Service as Unified Product Orchestrator"
    description: "We are consolidating a fragmented supplier connectivity landscape by building a unified Accommodation Service that acts as the order service orchestrator for all accommodation products. This service standardises inventory, pricing, and confirmation flows across all properties and supply types. By moving the front end and related services from separate Bedbank and Reservation Services to the Accommodation Service, we reduce complexity, accelerate development, and unlock cross-product features for both hotel and vacation rental offerings. This architectural change enables faster development and sets the foundation for unified product experiences across the platform."
