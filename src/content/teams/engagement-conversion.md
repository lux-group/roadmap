key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Reordering Information on Offer Page"
    description: "Some of the most important real estate on our website is at the top of our offer pages, leaving us large potential to improve the customer experience. We want to experiment with changing the location of information on the offer page by better displaying inclusions, reworking highlights, and featuring nearby locations. This is a high impact initiative with straightforward implementation that aims to lift conversion rate."
  - heading: "Map Search"
    description: "Our current map search greatly restricts user browsing behaviour and presents multiple pain points. The map gets significant views and is an important part of the booking journey, but the current implementation is too small and difficult to understand the value proposition without clicking into each offer. A complete UI overhaul is needed, mirroring improved features on the offer page. This will lift conversion and improve overall search experience."
  - heading: "Filters for All"
    description: "Booking.com has close to 3x the amount of filter categories we do. We need to bring in room-level filtering including wifi and bedding configuration, as well as additional property policies like pet policies. This improvement will help customers find exactly what they need and increase conversion through better filtering capabilities, particularly for more demanding travellers."
