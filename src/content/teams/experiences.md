  - heading: "Lounge Access and Global Transfers at Scale"
    description: "We are integrating with Collinson's global lounge network to unlock new loyalty experiences and lounge access at scale, adding 1,300 lounges worldwide with a 12-month generic QR code. This loyalty-first experience can be expanded for standalone and post-purchase offerings. Concurrently, we are building an API integration for Global Transfers that provides real-time quotes across 177 countries and 3,500 airports. Transfers currently represent 30% of total transaction value and 40% of gross margin across 27 countries, and this expansion unlocks significant inventory growth and monetization opportunity."

  - heading: "EML Co-Branded Card and Restaurant Ecosystem"
    description: "We are launching a co-branded debit card that provides exclusive discounts and benefits across restaurants, adding extra value to daily purchases. This new vertical includes a new vendor app and a dedicated restaurant ecosystem with Society points integration. The card provides benefits via discounts and points accrual for customers while establishing a new channel for supplier partnerships and revenue growth."

  - heading: "End-to-End Experience Discovery and Ranking"
    description: "We are executing a major redesign of the Experiences platform to improve customer discovery and drive conversion. This includes a new search engine powered by OpenText that enables discovery by intent, destination, and date with calendar search capabilities. We are rebuilding the entire Experiences journey with a new search results page, redesigned homepage with limited content approach and map-based landmarks (via AI), and a fully redesigned offer page. We are also implementing powerful search ranking with personalization models to increase offer discoverability, testing multiple ranking logics, and enabling more efficient in-flow experiences."
