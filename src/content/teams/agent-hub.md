initiatives_section_title: "Key Initiatives"
key_initiatives:
  - heading: "Société for Agents"
    description: "Agent Hub currently gives agents no reason to prioritize the platform over other suppliers, as commission is flat with no reward for loyalty. Introducing Société as Agent Hub's loyalty currency, agents earn one point for every dollar spent on the platform, aligning with industry models. Agents can track their growing points balance and work towards rewards over time. Société establishes the foundation of Agent Hub's loyalty system, shifting from a transactional booking tool to one that recognizes ongoing engagement and encourages consolidation of bookings."
  - heading: "Using Société Points to Drive Agent Behaviour"
    description: "A loyalty currency alone will not change behavior. We are building a dynamic incentive engine powered by Société, with personalized, data-led incentives optimized to target the right agent with the right motivation at the right moment. Agents receive clear incentives tied to specific milestones including first order bonuses, growth targets for developing agents, and performance challenges for high-volume sellers. By linking rewards directly to behavior, we move from occasional promotions to a system that builds consistent booking habits and sustained revenue growth."
  - heading: "Scalable Agency and Organisation Structure"
    description: "Agent Hub's current agency model is rigid and does not reflect how travel agencies actually operate. Enterprise partners lack organizational controls while independent agents lack flexibility. We are redesigning the agency and affiliation structure to support both enterprise networks and independent agents. Enterprise partners will gain head-office and store-level controls including permissions management, agent onboarding and performance visibility. Independent agents will gain self-service flexibility to manage agency affiliation, branding and contact details without manual support, allowing Agent Hub to scale efficiently."
